Tips

Setting up Bail Bloc is incredibly easy. Download the app and double click to install! This means you can install it at school, work, and all the computers on display at Best Buy. The more devices we can enlist in our cause, the better.

Bail Bloc shouldn’t interfere with anything you’re doing on your computer. If you notice any slowdown, simply reduce the CPU slider in the settings menu.

We’ve noticed that some Antivirus software needs to be told to ignore Bail Bloc. This makes sense - you wouldn’t want someone to mine cryptocurrency on your computer without your permission, right? In this case, you are electing to mine together with us to get people out on bail. Our software is totally open source and can be vetted by anyone. It couldn’t be safer.

Bail Bloc is a project of The New Inquiry in partnership with the Bronx Freedom Fund and the Columbia Graduate School of Journalism.
